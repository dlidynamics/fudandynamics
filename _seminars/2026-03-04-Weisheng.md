abstract: >
  We discuss the uniqueness of measure of maximal entropy, or more generally equilibrium states for geodesic flows on certain manifolds without conjugate points. Using the Patterson-Sullivan construction of measure of maximal entropy, we show Margulis’ asymptotic formula for the growth of the number of closed geodesics.
