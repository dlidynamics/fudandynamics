abstract: >
  Scaling scenery is a useful tool for studying fine-structure properties of fractal objects. Measures for which the scaling scenery flows satisfy a strong statistical property, called uniform scaling, appear to be geometrically more regular than general measures. In this talk, I shall present results on the scaling properties of stationary measures on self-conformal iterated function systems. The talk is based on joint works with B.Barany, A.Kaenmaki, A.Pyorala and Y.L. Wu.
